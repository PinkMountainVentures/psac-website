Insider Trails

Curated Desert Routes

Hand-selected trails from thousands of miles of personal experience in the Coachella Valley. These are the routes worth knowing.

Strenuous

San Jacinto Peak

11.6 mi 5,700 ft gain 7–10 hrs

Southern California's most dramatic summit. Take the Palm Springs Aerial Tramway to 8,516 ft, then hike through alpine wilderness to the 10,834 ft peak.

Moderate

Palm Canyon Trail

4.2 mi 450 ft gain 2–3 hrs

Wind through the world's largest California fan palm oasis. Ancient palms, seasonal stream crossings, and stunning canyon walls at every turn.

Moderate

Tahquitz Canyon

3.2 mi 360 ft gain 2 hrs

A sacred Agua Caliente Cahuilla site leading to a spectacular 60-foot waterfall. One of Palm Springs' most rewarding short hikes, hidden in plain sight.
