The pool is perfect.
And it's not enough.

Just past the pool deck, past the restaurants and the long golden afternoons, adventure is waiting.

Start My Adventure

The pool soothes. Adventure restores.

The pool takes the weight off. The trails, the canyons, the ridgelines you keep looking at over your sunglasses remind you of who you were before life got in the way. You've been feeling the pull since you arrived. Almost nobody knows where to start. That's exactly why we exist.

Find Yourself Here

Everyone Arrives Differently.

For the Curious

You need more than the pool. Not because it isn't perfect. It is. But something keeps pulling your eyes toward the mountains. You've been meaning to find out what's up there.

On the Trail

On a trail in a canyon, gradually climbing up a series of 7 waterfalls, each one pulling you further. Somewhere between the second and the third waterfall, you stop thinking about everything else. That's the version of you that is calling for adventure.

For Parents

You love Palm Springs. Your kids love it too, mostly. But between the adults-only pool and the restaurant without a kids menu, you start wondering if there's a version of this trip that's actually for all of you.

On the Trail

Your child stops every few feet to pick up a rock and hand it to you for safekeeping. Somewhere up the trail, they find their favorite boulder, climb it, and sit down to eat an apple. You linger. There's nowhere else to be.

For Athletes

You know how to move. You've put in the miles. What you're missing is terrain that matches you, people who get it without explanation, and a community built around the life you've chosen. That's here.

On the Trail

Past the canyon walls, scrambling up rock ledge after rock ledge, you turn around and catch it — Mount San Jacinto perfectly framed between the walls of the wash. The awe hits before the thought does. Skin rising, breath short. That's what the miles are for.
