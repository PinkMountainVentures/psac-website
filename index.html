Our Story

Insider Knowledge, Refined Expectations

Palm Springs Adventure Club was inspired by the incredible trails, palm trees, and waterfalls hidden in the mountains only moments from the luxury pools, fine dining, and elegant experiences of Palm Springs.

Palm Springs is rooted in indigenous history, steeped in Hollywood legend, and surrounded by opportunities for world class outdoor adventures and luxury après trail relaxation. We curate every trail, every moment, every pairing based on first-hand experience.

Based in Palm Springs and committed to finding the best of the best, we offer personalized adventure recommendations that match your fitness level and group size — then connect you with the finest recovery experiences the desert has to offer.
